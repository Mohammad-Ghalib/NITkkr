SPORTS FACILITIES

The Institute provides specialised infrastructure and professional coaches to guide them in numerous sports. It has specially designed courts for each sport such as a Standard 400 meter track, swimming pool, basketball courts, cricket ground, football ground, tennis courts, Badminton court, kabaddi courts, volleyball court, indoor sports facilities for gymnastics, table tennis, chess and more. The institute has professional coaches who help to not only improve the physical aspect of the sport but also the mental side of things.

SWIMMING POOL

NIT Kurukshetra now has a Swimming Pool of International Standards, 50 Mts. x 25 Mts. Size, with 90 Cm. depth at shallow end and 5 Mts. depth at deeper end. Apart from this it is equipped with Three Spring Boards for Diving at 3 Mts., 5 Mts. and 7 Mts height simultaneously. It is provided with Anti-wave Lane markers to conduct competitions with Portable Take off Boards with Fiber of Olympic type where 60 to 70 people can be accommodated at a time.
